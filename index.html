Click the shapes to start them moving.

If you resize the window you must refresh the page for the animation to be scaled

If they are clicked while moving (if you can catch them), there's a cool glitching that happens and they slowly move offscreen.

I made this with Javascript and the Snap.svg library. It is based on a screenprint I made, and I hope to emulate the experience/aesthetic of old puzzle platformer games.
